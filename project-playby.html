Project Overview

This social app connects local players, enabling them to upload, edit, and share custom videos. The video feed mimics short-style video formats like TikTok, with interactive features such as tagging, following, and content engagement.

Key challenges included implementing seamless video recording, editing features within the app, ensuring smooth playback, and integrating offline caching for better performance in low-connectivity areas.

  • Video recording and editing features integrated directly within the app
  • Custom video player for a smooth shorts-style experience
  • Player tagging and following functionality
  • Offline caching for video feed and uploads
  • Optimized image and video handling for better app performance
  • Clean architecture for scalable and maintainable app growth

Key Features

Video Recording
Record videos directly within the app using the camera package, with simple editing tools for trimming and filtering.
Player Tagging & Following
Tag players in your videos, follow their profiles, and stay updated with their latest content.
Offline Caching
Download and cache videos for offline viewing, ensuring uninterrupted content access even with a weak connection.

Technical Implementation

Architecture

The app follows a clean architecture pattern with separation between the UI, domain logic, and data layers. The video recording, editing, and playback functionalities are implemented as part of the presentation layer for easy accessibility.

Performance Optimization

Optimized video streaming, caching mechanisms, and image/video compression are used for smooth performance on low-end devices.

Challenges & Solutions

Integrated a lightweight video editing package that allows users to trim, cut, and apply basic effects directly within the app.

Implemented offline caching for videos, which ensures content can be viewed anytime, even without an internet connection.
